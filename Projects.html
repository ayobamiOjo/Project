Projects Overview

Course Chat

Are you a York University student facing challenges in connecting with your coursemates? Course Chat is designed to address these issues effortlessly, allowing prompt questions or participation in smaller groups without sharing personal information.

This initiative, expanded through the BEST program at York University, has been highly successful, offering access to various communication platforms created and managed by students.

Traders Ninja

Designed for Nigerian or African traders in Canada, Traders Ninja consolidates suppliers in one location, offering updated goods and price lists directly to your inbox. An interactive chat function facilitates negotiations, streamlining the shopping experience.
